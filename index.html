Mission

Why MedPath Connect?

As a Biomedical Sciences student, I experienced how difficult it can be to find meaningful opportunities in healthcare.

Research positions, volunteering experiences, shadowing, clinical exposure, and mentorship opportunities are often scattered across different websites, emails, and organizations.

MedPath Connect was created to bring these opportunities together in one place and make them easier for students to discover.
