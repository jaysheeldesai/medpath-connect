About

Why MedPath Connect exists

MedPath Connect was created to make healthcare opportunities easier to find. Many students struggle to locate research, clinical experience, volunteering, shadowing, and mentorship in one place. This platform brings those resources together so students can spend less time searching and more time growing.

The platform supports all students, while also including extra guidance for international students who may need to understand CPT, OPT, volunteering rules, or university-specific requirements.
